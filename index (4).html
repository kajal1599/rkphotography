A maternity shoot is all about proudly showing your baby bump to the world. So just make sure that the bump is in full shape when you get the shoot done. But the most important thing to consider is your comfort. Don't wait for the end days to get the shoot done as you might be uncomfortable during that time. For Maternity Photoshoot: Best time for maternity portraits is the last trimester (32-36 weeks / 8th month of Pregnancy is the best time for the Photoshoot. If you are carrying twins then 28-32 weeks / 7th month would be preferable.
